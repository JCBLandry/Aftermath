Packing List and Extra Bits


Whether you are brand new to LARP or a long returning player, knowing what to pack for a three day adventure can be daunting. But fear not, adventurer! We have created just for you a Survival Guide in the form of a printable PDF! The file includes a checklist for packing, some tips and reminders to consider before you load up and head out, as well as a list of common LARP terms and phrases you might hear while attending your event. We hope this Survival Guide will aid you in the darkness ahead...
