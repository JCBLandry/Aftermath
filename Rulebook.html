SOLAR: Aftermath is part of the SOLAR: Mississippi family. Each Mississippi chapter, including Aftermath, uses the Mississippi Chapter Rulebook Version 3.0, 2014 as its basis. In addition to this, Aftermath provides a rules supplement in addition to the main rulebook to cover unique features of the Aftermath multiverse.
